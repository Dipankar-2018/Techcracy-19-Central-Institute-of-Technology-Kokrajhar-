Robo Warfare
×

General Rules:

  • Each team can have a maximum of 4 members.
  • Each team must bring their own tools.
  • The Robot must weigh 10kg.
  • The dimension of the Robot should be 40 X 35 cm.
  • Ampflow, E-bike motors are not allowed.
  • The Robot can be Remote controlled or manual(Wire controlled).
  • AC power supply will only be provided.
  • Maximum power supply should be 12V.
  • In case of discrepancy, the final decision rests in the hands of the coordinators.
  • Deduction of points will be made for per cm increase in the dimensions.

GAMEPLAY:

  • Two teams will engage in a battle at a time. Each team will consist of one bot.
  • The team to score maximum points during the entire duration of the match or the team that knocks out the opponent wins the battle.
  • Members from different Colleges or institutes can form team showing their valid id cards.

Line Follower
×

General Rules:

  • Line Followers must be self-contained, and not externally operated by wire or by remote radio control during the race.
  • Except for the battery pack, the handler shall not make any addition, removal, replacement or change to the hardware of a Line Follower during a contest. It is however permissible to make minor repairs
  • A Line Follower shall not exceed 28 cm in overall length, 22 cm in overall width and 20 cm in overall height
  • Before commencement of normal racing, each racing team can test the racing environment on site
  • Within the given performance time, the handler is allowed to replace batteries and/or upload new program parameters to the Line Follower
  • External power supply is not allowed i.e. the power supply should be on board and should not be more than 12 volt.
